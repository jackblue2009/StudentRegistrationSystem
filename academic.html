Objectives

IT Programs:

Upon completion of the IT Programs the student will be able to:

  1. Plan, design, evaluate and implement a complete multimedia/web creation by utilizing the latest applications to produce highly interactive projects from conception to completion.
  2. Plan and develop an information system to be incorporated by any business establishment.
  3. Use the most popular programming platforms/applications/databases to develop a highly effective Business information system.
  4. Proactively solve problems and maintains the computer hardware and software resources.
  5. Carryout any necessary installations of required hardware and software components.
  6. Design and maintain local/wide area networks (LAN/WAN) to meet business requirements.

Commercial Studies/Office Management Programs:

  1. Conduct and manage day-to-day modern office duties using available office tools such as office computer/printers/and filing facilities.
  2. Acquire the appropriate communication skills that will make them able to deal with both management staff and customers alike.
  3. Engage in any secretarial duties to ensure the smooth-flowing and maximized success of that organization.
  4. Provide students with necessary commercial skills that will enable them to productively enhance the various business areas be it accounting, marketing, finance and e-business.
