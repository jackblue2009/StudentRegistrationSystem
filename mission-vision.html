Mission

To provide high international quality education and hands on training balanced between theory and practice. preparing students for the ever evolving demands of the labor market in the fields of Business, IT, and Engineering. Taking in to consideration work ethics students should posses to become successful contributions to the developments of the Bahraini Economy.


Vision

The College of Applied Studies targets in to be one of the leaders in the Applied Studies, a highly valued partner for labor market expertise and a catalyst for growth and development.


Objectives

Through various Business. IT, and Engineering programs it offers the college aims to enhance the knowledge and skills of school graduates enabling them to become the skilled work force to roll the wheels of economy forward.
